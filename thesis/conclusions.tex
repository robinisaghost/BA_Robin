\chapter{Conclusions and Future Work}
\label{ch:conclusions}

This chapter summarizes the central empirical findings of the ablation study discusses the underlying methodological limitations and outlines strategic directions for subsequent research. Section~\ref{sec:conc:summary} aggregates the core insights from all three research objectives. Section~\ref{sec:conc:limitations} assesses the architectural and structural boundaries of the evaluated frameworks, and Section~\ref{sec:conc:futurework} establishes clear vectors for future investigative work.

% ================================================================
\section{Summary of Findings}
\label{sec:conc:summary}
% ================================================================

\begin{planned}
    \item Conclusion regarding the systematic time shift anomaly confirming that modifying the optimization criteria alone does not resolve the predictive delay.
    \item Identification of the fundamental tracking information deficit where univariate data structures lack access to exogenous causal factors.
    \item Summary of Objective 1 indicating that bounded lag and soft dynamic time warping criteria expand temporal variance without improving underlying shape alignment.
    \item Summary of Objective 2 establishing direct multi step sequence supervision as the superior methodology for continuous trajectory prediction.
    \item Summary of Objective 3 demonstrating the clear performance advantage of the specialized recurrent binary event classifier over standard regression thresholding.
\end{planned}

% ================================================================
\section{Methodological Limitations}
\label{sec:conc:limitations}
% ================================================================

\begin{planned}
    \item Acknowledgment of the strictly univariate constraint which mathematically bounds the predictive capacity during sudden metabolic fluctuations.
    \item Critical assessment of the short median advance warning window achieved by the primary classification architecture.
    \item Evaluation of the severe dataset class imbalance which restricts overall precision metrics and induces false alarm sequences.
\end{planned}

\section{Future Work}
\label{sec:conc:futurework}
% ================================================================

\begin{planned}
    \item Proposal to integrate multimodal tracking sequences including logged insulin administration and carbohydrate consumption to eliminate the information deficit.
    \item Recommendation to evaluate expanded bounded lag optimization windows to fully encompass the observed systematic delay component.
    \item Suggestion to implement specialized focal loss functions to directly address minority class representation during neural network training.
    \item Perspective on transitioning to leave one out population configurations to measure cross patient generalizability.
\end{planned}
